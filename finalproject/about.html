Who We Are

NaijaStack is a curated directory dedicated to celebrating and amplifying Nigerian tech startups. We believe Africa's digital future is being built right here in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt and beyond — and the world deserves to know about it.

From fintech giants processing billions in transactions to agritech innovators feeding millions — Nigerian startups are solving real problems with world-class technology. NaijaStack puts them all in one place.
